The Epochs of the Eternal City

The Roman Kingdom
753 BC – 509 BC

The legendary era starting with Rome's foundation by Romulus. During this period, Rome was ruled sequentially by seven monarchs who established its earliest religious, political, and social institutions.

The Roman Republic
509 BC – 27 BC

Prompted by the overthrow of tyranny, Rome instituted a representative governance system guided by the Senate, consuls, and assemblies, managing immense territorial expansion before fracturing into civil wars.

The Roman Empire
27 BC – 476 AD

Initiated under Caesar Augustus, this period centralizing absolute political authority under an Emperor. It fostered the Pax Romana, unmatched architectural engineering, and eventual division into East and West blocks.
