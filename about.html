About the Project

This educational portal is dedicated to archiving the architectural triumphs, cultural transformations, and historical trajectories of classical antiquity.

Our focus maps across three specific turning points: the foundational Monarchy, the representative Roman Republic shaped by the Senate, and the eventual transition into the absolute rule of the Roman Empire initiated under Caesar Augustus in 27 BC.

Quick Timeline
  • 753 BC: Founding of Rome
  • 509 BC: Establishment of Republic
  • 27 BC: Rise of the Empire
  • 476 AD: Fall of the Western Empire
